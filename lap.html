About Loan Against Property

A Loan Against Property (LAP) is a secured loan where you pledge your residential or commercial property as collateral to avail funds. Borrowww offers LAP with:

  • Loan amounts up to 70% of property value
  • Interest rates starting from 8.5% p.a.
  • Flexible repayment tenure up to 15 years
  • Minimal documentation and quick processing
  • No restrictions on end-use of funds

Eligibility Criteria

Individuals

  • Age: 21-65 years
  • Minimum income: ₹25,000/month
  • CIBIL score: 650+

Businesses

  • Business vintage: 3+ years
  • Profitability for last 2 years
  • Property ownership documents

Documents Required

Identity & Address Proof

  • PAN Card
  • Aadhaar Card
  • Passport/Driving License
  • Latest Utility Bills

Income & Property Proof

  • Last 6 months bank statements
  • Salary slips/ITR for 2 years
  • Property papers (Title Deed)
  • Latest property tax receipt
