About Home Loans

A home loan helps you purchase, construct, or renovate your dream home. Borrowww offers home loans with:

  • Flexible repayment tenure up to 30 years
  • Balance transfer and top-up options

Home Loan FAQs

What is the maximum loan amount?

You can get up to ₹10 crores depending on your income, property value, and other factors.

What is the processing fee?

Processing fee ranges from 0.5% to 1% of loan amount, with a maximum of ₹25,000.

Can I get tax benefits?

Yes, you can claim tax deductions under Section 24 (up to ₹2 lakhs) and Section 80C (up to ₹1.5 lakhs).
