Balance Transfer

Reduce your EMIs by transferring your existing home loan to Borrowww at lower interest rates.

About Balance Transfer

A home loan balance transfer allows you to shift your existing home loan from your current lender to Borrowww to take advantage of lower interest rates and better terms. Benefits include:

  • Lower interest rates (up to 2% reduction possible)
  • Reduced EMI burden
  • Option to increase loan amount (top-up)
  • Better customer service
  • Flexible repayment options

When to Consider Balance Transfer

Financial Benefits

  • Current rate is 1%+ higher than market
  • Want to reduce monthly EMI
  • Need additional funds (top-up)

Service Benefits

  • Poor service from current lender
  • Frequent rate hikes
  • Need more flexible terms

Eligibility Criteria

Loan Requirements

  • Minimum 12 months repayment history
  • No defaults in last 12 months
  • Property papers in order

Borrower Requirements

  • CIBIL score: 700+
  • Stable income source
  • Valid property insurance
